About the game

The player controls a Protoss base thatâ€™s sent to a planet to erradicate the Zerg plague. The game is similar to the original Starcraft; to win you must defeat the enemy main base, that will be shown on the map, while protecting your own base and most importantly, the "Zerg Sample", the building near the player's starting location. Loosing it means loosing the game.

Upon destroying the enemy main base, Kerrigan will appear and attack the player. Defeat her before she gets to the sample or all hope of cleansing the planet will be lost!
